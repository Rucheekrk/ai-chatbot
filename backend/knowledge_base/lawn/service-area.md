## Service Area

We serve Greenfield, Maple Park, Northside, and Westbrook within 12 miles of our office at 218 Oak St.

A handful of streets just past the Westbrook boundary are evaluated case-by-case based on drive time and crew schedule.

## Neighborhoods We Serve

Primary service area (no travel fee):
- Greenfield (zip codes 30301, 30302)
- Maple Park (zip codes 30303, 30304)
- Northside (zip codes 30305, 30306)
- Westbrook (zip codes 30307, 30308)

Extended service area (additional travel fee may apply):
- Ridgewood Heights
- Elmwood Estates
- Cedar Bluff
- Lakewood Crossing

If you are unsure whether your address is in our service area, contact us and we will confirm within 1 business day.

## Travel Fees

- Within 12 miles of 218 Oak St: no travel fee
- 12–18 miles: $15 travel fee per visit
- Beyond 18 miles: evaluated case-by-case, contact us for a quote

## Scheduling and Response Time

- New customer inquiries are responded to within 1 business day
- First visit can typically be scheduled within 3–5 business days of sign-up
- Recurring appointments are locked in on a fixed weekly or biweekly schedule
- You will receive a reminder text or email the day before each scheduled visit

## Service Hours

- Monday through Friday: 7:00 AM – 6:00 PM
- Saturday: 8:00 AM – 4:00 PM
- Sunday: No service

## FAQ — Service Area
Q: What areas do you service? Do you service my neighborhood? Are you available in my area?
A: Our primary service area covers Greenfield, Maple Park, Northside, and Westbrook. Extended coverage includes Ridgewood Heights, Elmwood Estates, Cedar Bluff, and Lakewood Crossing. Contact us if your address is not listed and we will confirm within 1 business day.

## FAQ — Weekend Hours
Q: Do you work on weekends? Are you available on Saturdays? When are you open?
A: Yes, we work Saturdays 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM. We do not service on Sundays. Weekday hours are Monday through Friday, 7:00 AM to 6:00 PM.
