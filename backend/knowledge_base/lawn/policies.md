## Scheduling and Appointments

Specialized jobs (mixed grass types, steep terrain, large commercial properties, custom landscaping) are quoted by the owner after a site visit. We schedule those within 1 business day of the request.

Crew arrives in a 30-minute window. Service continues in light rain; heavy rain or thunderstorms reschedule to the next available day.

New customers receive a free yard assessment before the first visit to determine grass type, lot size, and any special requirements.

## Cancellation Policy

We require at least 24 hours notice to cancel a scheduled appointment.

- Cancellations made 24+ hours in advance: no charge
- Cancellations made less than 24 hours in advance: a $25 late cancellation fee applies
- No-shows (crew arrives and cannot access the property): billed at full service rate

To cancel an appointment, contact us by phone or email. Online cancellation through the app is coming soon.

## Reschedule Policy

We understand plans change. Rescheduling is free with at least 24 hours notice.

- Rescheduling requests are accommodated based on crew availability
- We will offer the next 2–3 available time slots for you to choose from
- Weather-related reschedules are always free and rescheduled automatically to the next available day

Online rescheduling with real-time calendar availability is coming soon. For now, contact us directly and our team will find you a new time.

## Payment Policy

- Payment is due within 48 hours of service completion
- We accept credit card, debit card, Venmo, and check
- Invoices are sent via email after each visit
- Recurring customers can set up auto-pay for convenience
- A $15 late fee applies to invoices unpaid after 7 days

## Weather Policy

- Service continues in light rain or overcast conditions
- Heavy rain, thunderstorms, or lightning: service is rescheduled to the next available day
- We monitor the forecast and will notify you by text or email if your visit needs to move
- No charge for weather-related reschedules

## Property Access

- Please ensure gate codes or access instructions are on file before your scheduled visit
- If the crew cannot access the property, the visit may be skipped and billed as a no-show
- Dogs should be secured indoors during service for safety of the crew and your pet
