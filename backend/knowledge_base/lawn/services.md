## Standard Mowing Service

Standard mowing service includes:
- Mowing at the appropriate height for the grass type (2.5"–3.5" for fescue, 1"–2" for bermuda)
- Edging along walks, driveways, and bed lines
- String-trimming around trees, poles, and fences
- Blowing clippings off all hard surfaces

We handle moderate slopes (up to ~15°). Steeper terrain requires an on-site assessment.

Available as weekly or biweekly visits. Same crew assigned to your property each visit for consistency.

## Spring Cleanup Service

Spring cleanup is a one-time seasonal service performed in early spring before the growing season begins.

What's included:
- Removal of dead leaves, branches, and winter debris from lawn and beds
- Dethatching to remove the layer of dead grass buildup
- Bed edging to redefine clean lines along driveways and walkways
- Light pruning of ornamental shrubs and bushes
- First mow of the season at the correct height for your grass type
- Blowing all clippings and debris off hard surfaces

Spring cleanup is recommended before starting a weekly or biweekly mowing plan.

## Fall Leaf Removal Service

Fall leaf removal is a one-time seasonal service performed in late fall after peak leaf drop.

What's included:
- Blowing and raking all leaves from lawn, beds, and around trees
- Bagging all collected leaves
- Haul-away included — no pile left behind
- Final mow of the season to winterize the lawn at the correct height
- Blowing debris off all hard surfaces

Multiple visits can be scheduled if needed depending on leaf volume.

## Mulch Installation

Mulch installation is available as a standalone service or as an add-on to spring cleanup.

What's included:
- Delivery of premium hardwood or pine bark mulch
- Spreading mulch evenly across all designated beds (2–3 inch depth recommended)
- Bed edging before installation if requested
- Clean-up of all excess material

Pricing is per yard of mulch installed. Contact us for a custom quote based on bed size and quantity needed.

## Add-On Services

The following services can be added to any mowing plan or seasonal service:

- **Aeration** — Core aeration to reduce soil compaction and improve root growth. Recommended once per year in fall for fescue, spring for bermuda.
- **Overseeding** — Spreading grass seed after aeration to fill bare patches and thicken the lawn. Best done alongside aeration.
- **Fertilization** — Seasonal fertilizer application to promote healthy growth and color. We use slow-release granular fertilizer suited to your grass type.
- **Bed weeding** — Hand-pulling weeds from flower beds and landscape areas. Available as a one-time or recurring add-on.

Contact us to add any of these to your service plan. Add-on pricing is quoted based on yard size and frequency.
